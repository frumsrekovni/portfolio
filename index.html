My Timeline
  • Grew up watching Xena: Warrior Princess

After an entire elementary school worth of halo and ice-hockey I entered a language upper secondary school with a non-existent retention of my spanish classes because it was the only local school that offered university preparing math classes.
  • 18 years old
    With language school done there is nothing more I want to do but to not learn formulas for a second. Nepotism is a virtue.. so I quickly found myself as a traffic flagger for a construction company, which in reality also meant construction fence builder. Never in my life have I gained this much appreciation toward air-conditioning.
  • University
    Entered Linköping University for Computer Science. The work ethic and discipline required here swiftly knocked me to the ground. I grit my teeth through building entire operative systems in C, building a little machine learning virtual rocket ship that needs to learn to hover by itself and often moving literal ones and zeros in order to test microchips. This was my introduction to the world of programming and computer science and it was equal parts amazingly cool and amazingly laborious.
  • Flashback
    With university done there is nothing more I want to do but to not learn formulas for a second. I ended up working front desk for a hostel which made me meet more people in a few years than I have collectively met in my entire life. Good for developing people skills but in the end did not feel like my calling.
  • Git Committing
    With proper self-reflection and consciously dusting of my feelings built up towards programming I eventually started actively code again. Programming was in my mind one and the same with computer science courses. Accepting the simple reality that it's ok to program anything that you want and by your own volition is very freeing and I've been running on this high since the start of 2022 just having fun trying to git commit every day as per this blogpost
